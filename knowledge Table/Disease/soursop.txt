Fruit: Soursop

Diseases:
1. Anthracnose
   - dark sunken lesions
   - fruit shriveling
   - soft rot on ripe fruit
2. Fruit Borer Damage
   - holes in green fruit
   - brown internal tunnels
   - premature fruit drop
3. Leaf Spot
   - brown or tan circular spots
   - yellow margins
   - scattered lesions
4. Root Rot
   - wilted young leaves
   - poor new growth
   - soft black roots
5. Stem Blight
   - dark elongated stem lesions
   - branch dieback
   - cracking bark
6. Pink Disease
   - pink fungal formation
   - drying branches
   - bark splitting
7. Phytophthora Fruit Rot
   - soft water-soaked spots
   - white fungal growth
   - rapid fruit decay
8. Sooty Mold
   - black leaf coating
   - sticky honeydew layer
   - reduced photosynthesis
