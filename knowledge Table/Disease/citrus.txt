Fruit: Citrus

Diseases:

1. Citrus Canker
   - raised brown lesions with yellow halo
   - corky scabs on fruit skin
   - leaf drop in severe cases

2. Greasy Spot
   - yellow blister-like spots
   - dark greasy appearance on underside
   - premature leaf fall

3. Citrus Scab
   - corky wart-like bumps on fruit
   - raised scabs on young leaves
   - distorted fruit shape

4. Melanose
   - small dark rough spots
   - scabby surface texture
   - affected areas do not enlarge

5. Phytophthora Root Rot
   - gummy exudate around trunk base
   - yellowing canopy
   - poor fruit set

6. Sooty Mold
   - black powdery coating
   - caused by insect honeydew
   - reduced photosynthesis

7. Citrus Tristeza Virus
   - stem pitting
   - vein clearing
   - stunted tree growth
