Fruit: Mango

Diseases:

1. Anthracnose
   - black sunken lesions on fruit
   - rapid fruit rot after harvesting
   - dark spots on leaves and flowers

2. Powdery Mildew
   - white powdery layer on leaves and flowers
   - distorted new leaves
   - premature fruit drop

3. Bacterial Black Spot
   - raised black lesions with cracking
   - oily appearance around spots
   - streaks on twigs and stems

4. Mango Scab
   - corky brown spots on fruit skin
   - raised lesions on leaves
   - scabby patches forming clusters

5. Alternaria Leaf Spot
   - irregular brown patches on leaves
   - yellow margins around spots
   - premature leaf drop

6. Mango Dieback
   - drying of young shoots
   - dark sunken patches on bark
   - wilting of leaves starting at tips

7. Stem-End Rot
   - dark rot starting at fruit stem
   - fruit softening during ripening
   - sour smell from infected area

8. Sooty Mold
   - black powdery growth on leaf surface
   - caused by insect honeydew
   - reduced photosynthesis
