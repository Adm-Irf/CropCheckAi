Fruit: Banana

Diseases:

1. Black Sigatoka
   - elongated dark streaks on leaves
   - yellow halos around lesions
   - premature leaf drying from the edges

2. Fusarium Wilt
   - yellowing and wilting of older leaves
   - brown vascular discoloration inside pseudostem
   - plant collapsing from the base upward

3. Banana Bunchy Top Virus
   - narrow upright leaves forming a bunchy appearance
   - dark green streaks on leaf veins and petioles
   - severe stunting of plant growth

4. Crown Rot
   - soft brown tissue around the crown
   - black or dark fungal growth on crown
   - foul rotting smell during ripening

5. Anthracnose
   - small sunken black spots on fruit skin
   - rapid softening and decay during ripening
   - pinkish spore masses on infected areas

6. Banana Freckle
   - tiny brown specks scattered across the peel
   - roughened peel texture
   - specks merging into irregular blotches

7. Moko Bacterial Wilt
   - sudden leaf wilt and yellowing
   - bacterial ooze when stem or fruit is cut
   - brown streaking inside fruit pulp

8. Fruit Rot
   - soft watery patches on skin
   - rapid discoloration
   - sour fermented smell
