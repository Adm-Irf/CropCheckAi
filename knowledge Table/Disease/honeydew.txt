Fruit: Honeydew

Diseases:
1. Powdery Mildew
   - white powder patches on leaves
   - reduced fruit sugar content
   - yellowing of older leaves
2. Downy Mildew
   - angular yellow leaf spots
   - grey fuzz on underside
   - rapid leaf collapse
3. Anthracnose
   - dark circular fruit spots
   - expanding sunken lesions
   - drying leaf lesions
4. Mosaic Virus
   - mottled yellow-green patterns
   - distorted leaf shape
   - stunted vine growth
5. Gummy Stem Blight
   - gummy ooze from stem cracks
   - brown leaf spots
   - vine dieback
6. Fruit Blotch
   - irregular dark rind spots
   - water-soaked patches
   - cracking fruit surface
7. Fusarium Wilt
   - yellowing lower leaves
   - brown vascular streaks
   - vine collapse
8. Sunscald
   - pale bleached rind areas
   - hard leathery patches
   - scorched streaks
