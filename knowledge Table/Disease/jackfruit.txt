Fruit: Jackfruit

Diseases:
1. Rhizopus Fruit Rot
   - soft watery rot on fruit
   - rapid collapse of flesh
   - white cottony fungal growth
2. Anthracnose
   - dark sunken spots
   - spreading black lesions
   - fruit surface cracking
3. Pink Disease
   - pink fungal coating on branches
   - twig dieback
   - bark cracking
4. Fruit Borer Damage
   - small entry holes
   - frass inside fruit
   - internal fruit decay
5. Stem Rot (Phytophthora)
   - brown stem lesions
   - water-soaked patches
   - stem collapse
6. Bacterial Blight
   - water-soaked leaf spots
   - yellow margin lesions
   - leaf drop
7. Fruit Bronzing
   - bronze-colored patches
   - rough fruit surface
   - uneven ripening
8. Alternaria Leaf Spot
   - brown leaf spots
   - concentric rings
   - premature leaf fall
