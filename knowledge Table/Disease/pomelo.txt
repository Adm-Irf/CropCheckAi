Fruit: Pomelo

Diseases:
1. Citrus Canker
   - raised corky lesions
   - yellow halos
   - premature leaf drop
2. Greasy Spot
   - yellow leaf blisters
   - dark oily underside
   - early defoliation
3. Phytophthora Rot
   - soft brown rind lesions
   - water-soaked patches
   - fruit breakdown
4. Citrus Scab
   - wart-like bumps
   - corky raised spots
   - distorted fruit
5. Melanose
   - tiny scabby brown dots
   - roughened rind
   - non-expanding lesions
6. Leaf Miner Damage
   - silver winding trails
   - curled young leaves
   - leaf thinning
7. Sooty Mold
   - black powdery coating
   - sticky honeydew layer
   - photosynthesis reduction
8. Nutrient Chlorosis
   - pale yellow leaves
   - green veins remaining
   - poor fruit formation
