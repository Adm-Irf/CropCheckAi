Fruit: Passionfruit

Diseases:
1. Brown Spot
   - brown circular leaf lesions
   - yellow margins
   - scattered spotting
2. Fusarium Wilt
   - wilting vines
   - brown vascular streaks
   - plant collapse
3. Anthracnose
   - sunken dark fruit spots
   - softening of infected areas
   - post-harvest rot
4. Septoria Leaf Spot
   - small grey leaf spots
   - dark borders
   - premature leaf drop
5. Mosaic Virus
   - mottled yellow-green leaves
   - leaf distortion
   - reduced vine vigor
6. Fruit Rot (Phytophthora)
   - water-soaked fruit patches
   - white fungal growth
   - rapid rot
7. Alternaria Blight
   - brown expanding leaf patches
   - dry lesion centers
   - leaf curling
8. Root Rot
   - yellow vine canopy
   - stunted growth
   - black soft roots
