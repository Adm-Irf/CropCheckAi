Fruit: Longan

Diseases:
1. Anthracnose
   - black sunken fruit spots
   - dry necrotic patches
   - post-harvest rot
2. Powdery Mildew
   - white powder on young leaves
   - distorted shoots
   - reduced flowering
3. Leaf Blight
   - brown leaf lesions
   - yellow borders
   - premature leaf drop
4. Sooty Mold
   - black sooty coating
   - sticky leaf surface
   - reduced photosynthesis
5. Fruit Borer Damage
   - small holes on fruit skin
   - internal tunneling
   - premature fruit drop
6. Root Rot
   - wilted canopy
   - stunted growth
   - dark soft roots
7. Pink Disease
   - pink fungal crust
   - branch dieback
   - cracked bark
8. Algal Leaf Spot
   - rusty orange spots
   - raised circular patches
   - slow leaf weakening
