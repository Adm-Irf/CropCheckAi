Fruit: Watermelon

Diseases:
1. Anthracnose
   - dark circular fruit spots
   - scorched leaf lesions
   - vine decline
2. Fusarium Wilt
   - one-sided vine wilting
   - brown internal stem streaks
   - collapsed vines
3. Downy Mildew
   - yellow angular leaf spots
   - grey fuzzy underside
   - rapid defoliation
4. Powdery Mildew
   - white powder patches
   - leaf yellowing
   - reduced fruit size
5. Gummy Stem Blight
   - gummy ooze from stem cracks
   - brown leaf spots
   - vine dieback
6. Bacterial Fruit Blotch
   - dark water-soaked rind spots
   - spreading blotches
   - fruit cracking
7. Mosaic Virus
   - mottled leaf patterns
   - leaf distortion
   - stunted vines
8. Blossom-End Rot
   - sunken dark lesion at fruit end
   - leathery texture
   - calcium deficiency-related
