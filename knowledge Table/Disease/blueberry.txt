Fruit: Blueberry

Diseases:
1. Anthracnose
   - soft berry rot
   - salmon-colored spore masses
   - shriveled berries
2. Mummy Berry Disease
   - tan shriveledberries
   - wilted new shoots
   - fruit drop
3. Botrytis Rot
   - grey fuzzy mold
   - soft watery berries
   - stem-end softening
4. Leaf Spot
   - brown circular leaf spots
   - dark edges
   - scattered lesions
5. Powdery Mildew
   - white coating on leaves
   - reddened leaf tips
   - reduced vigor
6. Root Rot
   - stunted bushes
   - yellowing leaves
   - rotting fibrous roots
7. Phytophthora Blight
   - stem darkening
   - wilting shoots
   - canopy collapse
8. Canker Disease
   - sunken stem lesions
   - branch dieback
   - cracked bark
