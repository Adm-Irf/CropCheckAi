Fruit: Papaya

Diseases:

1. Papaya Ringspot Virus
   - mosaic patterns on leaves
   - ring-shaped spots on fruit skin
   - distorted leaf growth

2. Anthracnose
   - black sunken spots on ripe fruit
   - rapid softening and rot
   - orange or pink spore masses

3. Phytophthora Blight
   - water-soaked lesions on fruit
   - white mold on infected areas
   - sudden wilting of leaves

4. Powdery Mildew
   - white powder on leaf surface
   - yellow patches on underside
   - leaf curling

5. Black Spot Disease
   - small dark circular spots
   - rough or scabby fruit surface
   - spots enlarging during ripening

6. Papaya Sticky Disease
   - sticky latex exudation from fruit
   - hard lumps under fruit skin
   - uneven ripening

7. Bacterial Canker
   - water-soaked streaks on stems
   - gummy exudate from lesions
   - wilted young leaves

8. Root Rot
   - yellowing of lower leaves
   - stunted growth
   - black mushy roots
