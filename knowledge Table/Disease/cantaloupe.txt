Fruit: Cantaloupe

Diseases:
1. Powdery Mildew
   - white powder on leaf surface
   - curling young leaves
   - early leaf death
2. Downy Mildew
   - yellow angular spots
   - grey underside growth
   - rapid defoliation
3. Anthracnose
   - sunken dark fruit lesions
   - leaf scorch patterns
   - vine decline
4. Fusarium Wilt
   - yellow one-sided vines
   - brown vascular tissue
   - plant collapse
5. Gummy Stem Blight
   - gummy stem cracks
   - dark leaf spots
   - vine death
6. Bacterial Wilt
   - sudden vine wilt
   - sticky ooze from stem
   - mottled leaf edge necrosis
7. Mosaic Virus
   - mosaic leaf patterns
   - twisted vines
   - reduced fruit size
8. Sunburn
   - pale bleached rind
   - hardened skin
   - surface cracking
