Fruit: Avocado

Diseases:
1. Anthracnose
   - small dark fruit spots
   - expanding sunken lesions
   - post-harvest rot
2. Phytophthora Root Rot
   - feeder root decay
   - yellowing canopy
   - branch dieback
3. Scab
   - rough corky spots on fruit
   - raised cracked lesions
   - blemished skin
4. Sunblotch Viroid
   - discolored streaks on fruit
   - mottled leaves
   - stunted tree growth
5. Cercospora Spot
   - angular brown leaf spots
   - yellow margins
   - premature leaf drop
6. Fruit Rot
   - soft brown flesh patches
   - fast deterioration
   - internal breakdown
7. Stem Canker
   - brown sunken stem lesions
   - drying branches
   - cracking bark
8. Sooty Mold
   - black powder coating
   - sticky leaf surface
   - reduced photosynthesis
