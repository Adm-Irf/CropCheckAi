Fruit: Breadfruit

Diseases:
1. Fruit Rot (Rhizopus)
   - soft watery patches
   - fast decay
   - white fungal strands
2. Leaf Spot
   - brown irregular leaf lesions
   - yellow halos
   - leaf shedding
3. Phytophthora Rot
   - dark water-soaked areas
   - fruit skin cracking
   - flesh collapse
4. Sooty Mold
   - black leaf surface
   - sticky coating
   - reduced light absorption
5. Rust
   - orange leaf pustules
   - powdery spores
   - early leaf fall
6. Stem Canker
   - sunken brown lesions
   - bark cracking
   - branch dieback
7. Nematode Damage
   - stunted growth
   - root swelling
   - small pale fruits
8. Fruit Borer Damage
   - entry holes on fruit
   - internal tunneling
   - rotting flesh
