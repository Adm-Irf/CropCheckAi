Fruit: Dragonfruit

Diseases:

1. Anthracnose
   - circular sunken lesions on stems or fruit
   - orange or pink spore masses
   - soft rot in advanced stages

2. Bacterial Soft Rot
   - watery softening of stem tissue
   - foul smell from decay
   - brown collapsing areas

3. Stem Canker
   - brown cracking lesions on stem
   - dry sunken patches
   - dark powdery fungal growth

4. Brown Spot Disease
   - small brown dots that expand
   - dry circular patches
   - cracking of older lesions

5. Botryosphaeria Rot
   - dark sunken stem spots
   - yellow margins around lesions
   - stem dieback

6. Alternaria Stem Rot
   - elongated dark lesions
   - yellowing around affected areas
   - stem drying

7. Sunburn
   - bleached yellow patches
   - rough thickened skin
   - cracks in severe cases

8. Nematode Damage
   - stunted growth
   - swollen or galled roots
   - reduced fruit size
