Fruit: Blackcurrant

Diseases:
1. Powdery Mildew
   - white leaf film
   - distorted new leaves
   - poor berry set
2. Leaf Spot
   - brown leaf specks
   - yellow borders
   - leaf shedding
3. Big Bud Mite Damage
   - swollen buds
   - poor flowering
   - stunted shoots
4. Reversion Virus
   - narrow leaves
   - reduced fruit clusters
   - pale leaf color
5. Botrytis Rot
   - grey fuzzy mold
   - soft berry collapse
   - stem-end rot
6. Canker
   - cracked stem lesions
   - branch dieback
   - bark peeling
7. Rust
   - orange spore patches
   - leaf yellowing
   - early defoliation
8. Root Rot
   - slow growth
   - wilted stems
   - dark decayed roots
