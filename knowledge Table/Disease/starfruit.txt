Fruit: Starfruit

Diseases:
1. Anthracnose
   - dark sunken spots
   - fruit edge browning
   - soft rot on ripe fruit
2. Leaf Spot
   - small brown patches
   - yellow edges
   - scattered lesions
3. Fruit Scab
   - corky scab-like bumps
   - rough raised patches
   - malformed fruit
4. Phytophthora Rot
   - water-soaked fruit lesions
   - white fungal growth
   - rapid collapse
5. Pink Disease
   - pink fungus on branches
   - bark cracking
   - twig death
6. Bacterial Leaf Blight
   - water-soaked streaks
   - leaf yellowing
   - premature drop
7. Fruit Borer Damage
   - holes in fruit
   - internal tunneling
   - browning flesh
8. Sunburn
   - pale yellow patches
   - hardened skin
   - surface cracking
