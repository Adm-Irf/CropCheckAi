Fruit: Mangosteen

Diseases:
1. Stem-End Rot
   - darkening at fruit stem
   - softening around attachment point
   - sour odor
2. Fruit Rot (Phytophthora)
   - brown soft patches
   - watery lesions
   - rapid decay
3. Pink Disease
   - pink fungal crust
   - twig dieback
   - bark splitting
4. Anthracnose
   - black sunken lesions
   - dry cracked patches
   - fruit surface blemishes
5. Black Spot
   - tiny scattered dark dots
   - roughened skin
   - non-expanding spots
6. Leaf Blight
   - brown necrotic areas
   - leaf curling
   - premature drop
7. Fruit Borer Damage
   - small holes in rind
   - brown tunnels inside
   - internal decay
8. Root Rot
   - stunted growth
   - yellow leaves
   - black decayed roots
