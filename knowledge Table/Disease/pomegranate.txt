Fruit: Pomegranate

Diseases:
1. Bacterial Blight
   - brown water-soaked leaf lesions
   - cracked fruit spots
   - blackening of twigs
2. Alternaria Fruit Rot
   - black sunken spots
   - dry cracked lesions
   - internal rot
3. Wilt Disease
   - sudden leaf drooping
   - brown vascular tissues
   - plant collapse
4. Powdery Mildew
   - white powder coating
   - distorted leaves
   - reduced fruit set
5. Fruit Cracking
   - split rind
   - exposed arils
   - uneven water uptake issues
6. Anthracnose
   - brown circular spots
   - pink spore masses
   - expanding lesions
7. Bark Canker
   - sunken dark stem lesions
   - gummy exudate
   - branch dieback
8. Fruit Borer Damage
   - holes in fruit rind
   - brown tunnels inside
   - drying arils
