Fruit: Coconut

Diseases:
1. Bud Rot
   - soft decaying growing point
   - foul smell
   - unopened spear leaf turning brown
2. Lethal Yellowing
   - yellowing of lower fronds
   - fruit drop
   - blackening of inflorescence
3. Stem Bleeding
   - reddish-brown fluid oozing
   - dark streaks on trunk
   - gradual decline of canopy
4. Leaf Spot
   - brown circular leaf lesions
   - yellow margins
   - scattered spotting
5. Root Wilt
   - drooping leaflets
   - small malformed nuts
   - slow growth
6. Ganoderma Butt Rot
   - decay at trunk base
   - fungal brackets near soil line
   - tree instability
7. Black-Headed Caterpillar Damage
   - chewed leaf surfaces
   - dried leaf tips
   - webbing on fronds
8. Nutrient Deficiency (Potassium)
   - orange leaf spotting
   - yellow edges
   - weak fruit set
