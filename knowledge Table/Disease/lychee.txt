Fruit: Lychee

Diseases:
1. Anthracnose
   - dark sunken skin lesions
   - cracked fruit surface
   - rapid fruit decay
2. Fruit Borer Damage
   - small exit holes
   - brown internal tunnels
   - spoiled flesh
3. Powdery Mildew
   - white powder on leaves
   - distorted new shoots
   - reduced fruit set
4. Red Algae Spot
   - reddish raised leaf patches
   - roughened surface
   - leaf yellowing
5. Leaf Blight
   - large brown patches
   - drying leaf tips
   - premature leaf fall
6. Stem Canker
   - cracked bark
   - sunken stem lesions
   - branch dieback
7. Sooty Mold
   - black fungal film
   - sticky leaves
   - weakened photosynthesis
8. Root Rot
   - slow growth
   - wilted new shoots
   - blackened roots
