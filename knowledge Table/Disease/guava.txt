Fruit: Guava

Diseases:
1. Anthracnose
   - black circular fruit spots
   - dry cracked lesions
   - ripening fruit rot
2. Wilt Disease
   - sudden leaf drooping
   - brown vascular tissues
   - plant collapse
3. Fruit Fly Damage
   - small puncture marks
   - brown soft patches
   - larvae tunneling
4. Rust
   - orange powdery pustules
   - leaf underside spots
   - premature leaf shedding
5. Canker
   - raised corky patches
   - cracking lesions
   - deformed fruit
6. Fruit Rot (Fusarium)
   - water-soaked fruit areas
   - color shifts to brown
   - softening flesh
7. Cercospora Leaf Spot
   - greyish circular spots
   - dark borders
   - leaf drop
8. Root Rot
   - yellow wilted canopy
   - stunted growth
   - black mushy roots
