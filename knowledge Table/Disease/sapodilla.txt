Fruit: Sapodilla

Diseases:
1. Anthracnose
   - black circular lesions
   - cracked skin patches
   - soft fruit rot
2. Leaf Spot
   - brown round spots
   - yellow edges
   - scattered lesions
3. Fruit Rot
   - brown rind softening
   - water-soaked areas
   - internal decay
4. Sooty Mold
   - black powder film
   - sticky leaf surface
   - weakened growth
5. Leaf Blight
   - large necrotic leaf patches
   - dried tips
   - premature leaf fall
6. Powdery Mildew
   - white powder spots
   - curled juvenile leaves
   - inhibited growth
7. Blossom-End Rot
   - dark lesion at fruit end
   - leathery patch
   - calcium imbalance signs
8. Root Rot
   - wilted young growth
   - poor nutrient uptake
   - blackened decayed roots
