Fruit: Rambutan

Diseases:
1. Anthracnose
   - black sunken fruit spots
   - necrotic patches on skin
   - rapid fruit decay
2. Pink Disease
   - pink fungal growth on branches
   - twig dieback
   - bark cracking
3. Powdery Mildew
   - white powder on leaves
   - distorted leaf growth
   - poor fruit set
4. Leaf Blight
   - brown irregular leaf patches
   - yellow edges
   - dried leaf tips
5. Stem Borer Damage
   - small holes on stems
   - frass around entry points
   - branch dieback
6. Fruit Rot (Fusarium)
   - soft fruit tissue
   - color change to brown
   - fungal smell
7. Sooty Mold
   - black powder on leaves
   - sticky honeydew
   - reduced photosynthesis
8. Dieback
   - drying branches
   - leaf wilting
   - brown discoloration on twigs
